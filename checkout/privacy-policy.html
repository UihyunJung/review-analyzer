Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Place Review Analyzer ("the Extension") is a browser extension that uses AI to analyze publicly visible reviews on Google Maps and Naver Place. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

  • Email address — Collected only if you create an account. Used solely for authentication.
  • Review text — Temporarily transmitted to our server for AI analysis. Not stored. Discarded immediately after processing.
  • Analysis results — Place name, URL, and AI-generated summary (aspect scores, highlights, warnings) are stored in your analysis history.
  • Usage count — We track how many analyses you perform per day to enforce free tier limits.
  • Device identifier — A randomly generated UUID stored locally in your browser. Used to track usage for non-authenticated users. Not linked to your identity.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history
  • Personal information beyond email (if registered)
  • Payment information (handled entirely by Paddle)
  • Original review text (never stored on our servers)

How We Use Your Data

  • AI Analysis — Review text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis. Anthropic's data retention policy applies to this processing.
  • Analysis History — Stored to let you view past analyses. Automatically deleted after 90 days.
  • Authentication — Managed by Supabase. Email is used only for login.
  • Payments — Processed by Paddle. We never see or store your payment details.

Data Retention

  • Analysis history: 90 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Account deletion: All associated data is deleted immediately upon request.
  • Usage data: Rolling daily counts, no long-term retention.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — AI analysis processing. Privacy Policy
  • Supabase — Authentication and database. Privacy Policy
  • Paddle — Payment processing. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — API hosting. Privacy Policy

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Upon deletion, all data is removed immediately.
