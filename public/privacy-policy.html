Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Place Review Analyzer ("the Extension") uses AI to analyze publicly visible reviews on Google Maps.

Data We Collect

  • Review text — Temporarily transmitted for AI analysis. Not stored.
  • Analysis results — Place name, URL, AI-generated summary stored in history.
  • Usage count — Daily analysis count for free tier limits.
  • Device identifier — Random UUID stored locally. Not linked to identity.
  • Email — Only if you use purchase restore. Used solely for subscription lookup.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Browsing history, personal information, payment details (handled by Paddle)
  • Original review text (never stored on servers)

Third-Party Services

  • Google (Gemini API) — AI processing. Terms of Service
  • Supabase — Database. Privacy Policy
  • Paddle — Payments. Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare — API hosting. Privacy Policy

Data Retention

  • Analysis history: 90 days, then auto-deleted.
  • Account deletion: All data removed immediately upon request.
